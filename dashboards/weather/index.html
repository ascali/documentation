Dashboards for a new generation of weather analytics

Geographic data is one of the most abundant forms of data available. With today’s prevalence of satellite data and cheap, distributed sensors, dashboards that show geographic trends with high granularity across multiple dimensions are now the status-quo rather than the exception.

For global-scale interactive maps, Plotly provides unparalled geo graphics that have the feature breadth of Python's Basemap library. With 16 geographic projections, vector rendering, and a streaming API, Plotly maps are used in mission critical applications for call centers, satellite trajectories, product tracking, the social sciences, weather forecasting, and online journalism.
